About Us

Delight Water Solutions ( Private) Ltd is a privately owned company established 2016 engaged in providing comprehensive solutions for Water purification & Waste water Management projects. We provide a Broad Range of Services, Supplies, Components and Accessories, besides wide range of customized offered solutions such as Wastewater Plant or rather Reengineering/ Upgrading/ Refurbishing an Existing plants, Operation & Maintenance Solutions, Chlorination, Spare Parts or Chemical Supplies.

Delight Water solutions was established to mainly service the household sector for fulfilling their drinking water requirement and supply various types of related equipment. Due to heightened market demand, Delight water solutions aggressively expanded into industrial market segments by expanding its products offering services that were lacking in the market place. Also We help our clients to reach the most possible optimum design, best technologies and operation techniques for fulfilling their requirements to reach.
