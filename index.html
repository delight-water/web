About Us

Delight Water Solutions ( pvt) Ltd. has expertise in successfully handling the filtration and purification needs of Commercial & Industrial Water plants. It also provides the cost effective services of transmission wastewater, its treatment, recycle, distribute to the industries and environment. Ancillary equipment and systems for water filtering, supply, storage, cooling, dispensing, recycling, and chemical used for purification are also part of our products offering. These systems can also be made available in customized specifications or configurations suit to individual requirements from regular household consumption to specialized industrial processes.
